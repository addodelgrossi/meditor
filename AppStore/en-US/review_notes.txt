Meditor requires no account. Editing, rendering, Quick Look, export, clipboard, and presentations work offline. Network access is used only when the reviewer explicitly chooses Publish or Unpublish.

To review the main workflow:
1. Launch the app and choose any built-in template.
2. Edit Mermaid source in the left pane and verify the live preview on the right.
3. Use the layout selector to switch between editor, split, and preview modes.
4. Use Export to preview and save SVG, retina PNG, or PDF, or copy a rich image to the clipboard.
5. Use Present to build a temporary multi-file deck and enter a full-screen presentation.
6. Use Publish to create an expiring view-only link, then use Diagram > Published Links to open or unpublish it.
7. Open Settings to review editor/export preferences, the configured share service, and the About & Legal section.
8. In Finder, select any .mmd or .mermaid file associated with Meditor and press Space to verify the rendered Quick Look preview. Invalid or empty files display their source instead.

All Mermaid rendering, including the Quick Look extension, is performed locally using the bundled JavaScript resource. The renderer has a Content Security Policy with connect-src 'none' and navigation is restricted to local file/about URLs. The Quick Look extension has no network entitlement.

The main app's network entitlement supports the explicit Publish feature. Publish sends diagram source, selected theme, a generated social preview image, and the selected expiry duration to the configured HTTPS share service. Unpublish sends the stored deletion token. No diagram content is transmitted during editing, rendering, Quick Look, export, clipboard, or presentation workflows.
