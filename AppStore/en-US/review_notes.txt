Meditor requires no account and has no network-based features.

To review the main workflow:
1. Launch the app and choose any built-in template.
2. Edit Mermaid source in the left pane and verify the live preview on the right.
3. Use the layout selector to switch between editor, split, and preview modes.
4. Use Export to save SVG, PNG, or PDF through the standard macOS save panel.
5. Open Settings to review editor/export preferences and the About & Legal section.

All Mermaid rendering is performed locally using the bundled JavaScript resource. WKWebView requires the network client entitlement for its sandboxed helper process, but the renderer has a Content Security Policy with connect-src 'none' and navigation is restricted to local file/about URLs. The app makes no network requests and does not transmit document content.
